About GLOBAL METALS

GLOBAL METALS is a leading scrap metal recycling company in Miami, dedicated to providing top-quality service and the best prices for your scrap metal.

We specialize in purchasing all types of ferrous and non-ferrous metals from individuals, businesses, and construction companies. Our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility makes us a trusted partner in metal recycling.

With our professional team and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure fair weighing, competitive pricing, and immediate payment for all your scrap metal.

Our Services

Comprehensive scrap metal solutions

Scrap Metal Purchase

We buy all types of metals: iron, steel, aluminum, copper, brass, stainless steel, electric cables, batteries, and old appliances.

Pickup Service

We offer pickup service for large quantities from factories, workshops, construction sites, and demolition projects.

Industrial Demolition

Dismantling of machinery, metal structures, factories, and warehouses. We recover metal for recycling.

Waste Management

Regular contracts with companies for periodic metal waste removal and recycling container placement.

Vehicle Scrap
